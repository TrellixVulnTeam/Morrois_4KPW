The Lincoln Thornton Manuscript

The most recent print facsimile of Lincoln Cathedral Library MS 91 is Derek Brewer and A. E. B. Owen, The Thornton Manuscript (Lincoln Cathedral Ms. 91) (Reprinted with Introd. Rev. ed. London: Scolar Press, 1977).

However, the most up-to-date and finer-grain list of contents can be found in Susanna Fein, "The Contents of Robert Thornton's Manuscripts," in Robert Thornton and His Books: Essays on the Lincoln and London Thornton Manuscripts, edited by Susanna Fein and Michael Johnston, pp. 13-65 (Woodbridge, Suffolk: York Medieval Press in association with The Boydell Press, 2014). The following listing, numbering, and brief descriptions of the various articles are drawn from Fein:

Booklet 1 (fols. 3-52; arts. 1-6); "the life of an ancient Worthy before that of a medi­eval one: King Arthur of Britain in the alliterative Morte Arthure" (Fein, 16):
  • Art. 1: The Prose Life of Alexander (fols. 1r-49r) 
  • Art. 2: Birth record for Thornton's grandson (fol. 49v)
  • Art. 3: Thunder Prognostications (fol. 5r-5v)
  • Art. 4: blank (fol. 51r))
  • Art. 5: Lamentacio Peccatoris (fol. 51va-52ra)
  • Art. 6: Sketches of knights and a horse (fol. 52v)
Booklet 2 (fols. 53-178; arts. 7-36); "one of the premier medieval collections of Middle English verse romance" (Fein, 17):
  • Art. 7: Scribal signature in English or French (fol. 53r)
  • Art. 8: The Alliterative Morte Arthure (fol. 53r-98v)
  • Art. 9: Latin verse (fol. 98v)
  • Art. 10: Octavian (fols. 98va—109rb)
  • Art. 11: Isumbras (fols. 109rb—114va)
  • Art. 12: Latin verse (fol. 114va)
  • Art. 13: Earl of Toulouse (fols. 114vb-122ra)
  • Art. 14: Life of Saint Christopher (fols. 122vb-129vb)
  • Art. 15: Sir Degreuant (fols. 130ra-139va)
  • Art. 16: Sir Eglamour of Artois (fols. 139va-147rb)
  • Art. 17: The Wicked Knight and the Friar (Miracle of the Virgin) (fols. 147ra-148rb)
  • Art. 18: Lyarde (fols. 148rb-149)
  • Art. 19: Thomas of Erceldoune's Prophecy (fols. 149va-153vb)
  • Art. 20: Awntyrs off Arthure (fols. 154r-161r)
  • Art. 21: Sir Perceval of Gales (fols. 161ra-176ra)
  • Art. 22: Thornton Toothache Charm (fol. 176rb)
  • Art. 23: Three Good Brothers Charm (version B) (fol. 176rb)
  • Art. 24: Latin Toothache Charm (fol. 176v)
  • Art. 25: Epistola Sancti Salvatoris (fol. 176v)
  • Art. 26: Charm invoking the cross for protection (fol. 176v)
  • Item 27: English prose, Occasions for the Latin Prayer for Deliverance (fols. 176v-177r)
  • Art. 28: Latin Prayer for Deliverance (fol. 177r)
  • Art. 29: Lady for Thy Joys Five (fol. 177v)
  • Art. 30: Prayer on Mary’s Five Joys (fols. 177v-178r)
  • Art. 31: A Meritorious Collect (fol. 178r)
  • Art. 32: Adoro te crucem (fols. 178r-178v)
  • Art. 33: Prayer on the Seven Gifts of the Holy Ghost (fol. 178v)
  • Art. 34: Sancta Maria mater Cristi (fol. 178v)
  • Art. 35: Omnipotens sempiterne Deus (fol. 178v)
  • Art. 36: O virtutum Domine (fol. 178v)
Booklet 3 (fols. 179-279; arts. 37-98); "religious devotions and treatises, often in prose" (Fein, 17)
  • Art. 37: In Nomine Patris et Filii et Speritus Sancti (Invocation of the Trinity) (fol. 119r)
  • Art. 38: Privity of the Passion (English translation of Pseudo-Bonaventure, Meditationes vitae Christi) (fols. 179r-189r)
  • Art. 39: Of All Things It Is the Best (written in a formal hand not Thornton’s) (fol. 189r)
  • Art. 40: William of Nassington, Tractatus de Trinitate et Unitate (English verse) (fols. 189r-191v)
  • Art. 41: Lord God Jesus Christ, God Almighty (fol. 191v)
  • Art. 42: Almighty God in Trinity (fol. 191v)
  • Art. 43: Lord God All-Wielding (fol. 191v)
  • Art. 44: Jesus That Died on the Rood for the Love of Me (fols. 191v-192r)
  • Art. 45: Jesus of Whom All True Love Springs (fol. 192r)
  • Art. 46: Richard Rolle, Oleum effusum in English (Version A) (fols. 192r-193v)
  • Art. 47: Richard Rolle, A Tale of Hampoles Temptation (fol. 193v)
  • Art. 48: Jesus the Son of the Glorious Virgin (fol. 193v)
  • Art. 49: Deus noster refugium (Prayer ascribed to Richard Rolle) (fol. 193v)
  • Art. 50: Ihesu nostra redempcio amor & desiderium (Hymn ascribed to Saint Ambrose) (fols. 193v-194r)

  • Art. 53: Richard Rolle, The Bee and the Stork (fol. 194r-194v)
  • Art. 54: Richard Rolle, A Woman Enclosed for Love of Christ (fols. 194v-195r)
  • Art. 55: Meliora sunt (Latin meditation ascribed to Richard Rolle) (fol. 195r)
  • Art. 56: O quam delectabile (Latin meditation ascribed to Richard Rolle) (fol. 195r)
  • Art. 57: Richard Rolle, Commentary on the Decalogue (Shorter Version) (fols. 195v-196r)
  • Art. 58: Richard Rolle, Seven Gifts of the Holy Spirit (fol. 196r-196v)
  • Art. 59: Ihesus Marie filius sit michi / clemens & propecius (fol. 196v)
  • Art. 60: Richard Rolle, Desire and Delight (fol. 196v)

  • Art. 62: English and Latin prose Tract on the Lord’s Prayer (fols. 209v-211r)
  • Art. 63: Jesus Christ Saint Mary’s Son (fol. 211ra-211va)

  • Art. 65: Ihesu criste goddes sun of heuen (Prayer to Christ) (fol. 212rb)
  • Art. 66: Adoro te piissime Ihesu qui / redimisti me (Latin verse meditation on the five wounds with prose prayer and collect) (fol. 212rb-212vb)
  • Art. 67: O crux frutex saluificus vino fonte rigatus (Latin verse meditation with prose antiphon and collect; extract from Saint Bonaventure’s Arbor vitae Christi) (fols. 212vb-213r)
  • Art. 68: Ihesus pie flos Marie peccatorum miserere Amen (Latin verse and prose invocation for mercy) (fol. 213r)
  • Art. 69: When Adam Delved (fol. 213r-213v)
  • Art. 70: Jesus Christ, Have Mercy on Me (fol. 213v)
  • Art. 71: John Gaytryge, Sermon (or Lay Folks’ Catechism) (fols. 213v-218v)
  • Art. 72: Jesus Thy Sweetness (fol. 219ra-219v)
  • Art. 73: Walter Hilton, Of Angels’ Song (fols. 219v—221v)
  • Art. 74: Richard Rolle, Thy Joy Be in the Love of Jesus (fol. 222r-222v)
  • Art. 75: Richard Rolle, All Vanities Forsake (fol. 222v)
  • Art. 76: Walter Hilton, Epistle on the Mixed Life (Shorter Version) (fols. 223r-229r)
  • Art. 77: Walter Hilton, Epistle of Salvation (extracted from The Scale of Perfection, Book I, chap. 44) (fols. 229v-230v)
  • Art. 78: Of all mankynde þat he made þat maste es of myghte (Alliterative Salutation to Saint John the Evangelist) (fols. 231r-233v)
  • Art. 79: Prayng es a gracyous gyfte of owre lorde godd (On Prayer) (fols. 233v-236v)
  • Art. 80: Holy Boke Gratia Dei (three parts in Lincoln: “On Prayer,” “On Grace,” and “Our Daily Work';” lacking fourth part, “Meditation on the Passion and of Three Arrows of Doomsday”) (fols. 237r-250v)
  • Art. 81: Revelation Shown to a Holy Woman (fols. 250v-258r)
  • Art. 82: Vulgate Psalm 50, concluded with a benediction (fol. 258r-258v)
  • Art. 83: Hymn Veni creator spiritus (variant ending) (fol. 258v)
  • Art. 84: Psalter of Saint Jerome with Office (uniquely abridged) (fols. 258v-270v)
  • Art. 85: Abbey of the Holy Ghost (Northern Version) (fols. 271r-276r)
  • Art. 86: First Age of Man’s Life (Extract from the Prick of Conscience, ll. 438-551) (fols. 276va-277ra)
  • Art. 87: Originally blank column with later trial alphabet (fol. 277rb)
  • Art. 88: Gaude flore virgenali (Salutation to Mary for her Seven Joys) (fol. 277va-277b)
  • Art. 89: Gaude virgo Mater Cristi (Salutation to Mary for her Five Joys) (fols. 277vb-278r)
  • Art. 90: Benedictio et Claritas et Sapiencia et graciarum accio (Hymn with versicle and collect) (fol. 278r)
  • Art. 91: Tuam crucem adoramus (Hymn on the Passion) (fol. 278r)
  • Art. 92: A Colecte of grete perdon vnto crist Ihesu / Domine Ihesu Criste Fili dei (fol. 278r)
  • Art. 93: Thornton misereatur mei dei / miserere mei deus  (fol. 278v)
  • Art. 94: Crucem coronam spiniam (Hymn on the arma Christi, with prose prayer and collect) (fol. 278v)
  • Art. 95: Ihesus Marie filius sit michi clemens / & propicius (fol. 278v)
  • Art. 96: Latin hymn on Christ’s side wound with concluding anthem and prose collect (fols. 278v-279r)
  • Art. 97: Earth upon Earth (Version B) (fol. 279r-279v)
  • Art. 98: For the Sciatica (medical recipe) (fol. 279v)
Booklet 4 (fols. 280-321; arts. 99-100); "the medical collection Liber de Diversis Medicinis" (Fein, 18)
  • Art. 99: Liber de Diversis Medicinis (fols. 280r-314v)
  • Art. 100: Fragmental herbal drawn from A Tretys of Diverse Herbis (fols. 315r—321v)
